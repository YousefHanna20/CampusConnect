About the Society

The Aperture Society is the premier hub for visual storytellers and photography enthusiasts at CampusConnect. Founded in 2012, we provide a collaborative space for students to explore the art of photography, from traditional film development to high-end digital retouching. Our mission is to document the vibrant life of our university while helping members build professional portfolios.
